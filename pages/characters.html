Ranjit, played by Marshall Manesh, is a recurring secondary character in How I Met Your Mother, known for being the taxi and limousine driver who assists the group of friends during multiple key moments in the series. Originally from Bangladesh, Ranjit stands out for his cheerful and helpful attitude, always willing to assist the protagonists in their adventures.

His first appearance is in the pilot episode, where he drives Ted Mosby to his first date with Robin Scherbatsky. Throughout the series, Ranjit becomes a frequent ally, especially during moments of celebration or important events. Although he is not directly part of the main group, his presence is indispensable, bringing comedic moments and being remembered for his warm "Hello!" with a distinctive accent.

Despite his secondary role, Ranjit is a beloved character by fans, thanks to his loyalty, good humor, and the comedic situations he finds himself in.

Victoria

Victoria, played by Ashley Williams, is one of Ted Mosby's most important love interests in How I Met Your Mother. Introduced in the first season, Victoria is a talented pastry chef whom Ted meets at a wedding, and their relationship quickly becomes one of the most significant in the series.

Victoria stands out for her sweetness, kindness, and a genuine connection with Ted. Unlike other characters in Ted's life, her relationship with him seems to have a promising future from the start. However, circumstances complicate their story, as Victoria receives an offer to study pastry in Germany, leading to a long-distance relationship that eventually fails.

Throughout the series, Victoria reappears several times, showing that although the initial spark between her and Ted is still present, personal complications and the divergent paths of their lives prevent them from being together permanently.

Victoria is remembered by fans as one of Ted's most beloved girlfriends, with many considering her an ideal candidate to be "the mother." Her likable personality and sincere relationship with Ted make her one of the most cherished secondary characters in the series.

Carl the Bartender

Carl the Bartender, played by Joe Nieves, is a recurring character in How I Met Your Mother. Carl is a secondary character who appears at key moments, serving drinks and often interacting casually with Ted, Barney, Marshall, Lily, and Robin.
